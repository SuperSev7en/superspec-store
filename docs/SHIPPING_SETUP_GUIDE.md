## Shipping Overview

### How Shipping Works in Your Store
Your store uses **flat-rate shipping** that you configure. Unlike some platforms, you DON'T need to integrate with complex shipping services to get started.

**Simple Flow:**
1. Customer pays for shipping during checkout
2. You package the order
3. You buy shipping label separately
4. You enter tracking number in admin panel
5. Customer gets shipping notification email

|--------|------|---------------|-------------|

| **Express** | $14.99 | 2-3 business days | Rush orders |

## Basic Setup (No Third-Party Service)

### What You Need to Start
1. **Packaging Materials**
   - Boxes or mailers appropriate for your products
   - Bubble wrap or tissue paper for protection
   - Packing tape
   - Shipping labels (printed)

2. **Scale** (optional but recommended)
   - Kitchen scale works for small items
   - Postal scale for accurate weights
   - Helps avoid over/under-paying for shipping

3. **Printer**
   - Standard printer for shipping labels
   - Thermal printer (optional, for high volume)

### Shipping Without Third-Party Services

#### Option 1: Post Office (USPS)
1. **Package your order**
2. **Go to local post office**
3. **Tell them destination and weight**
4. **Pay for shipping**
5. **Get tracking number**
6. **Enter tracking in admin panel**

**Pros:** No setup required, available everywhere
**Cons:** Can be busy, limited hours, retail prices

#### Option 2: Online Postage (Recommended)
1. **Package your order**
2. **Weigh and measure package**
3. **Buy postage online**
4. **Print shipping label at home**
5. **Drop off package or schedule pickup**
6. **Enter tracking in admin panel**

**Pros:** Discounted rates, print at home, schedule pickups
**Cons:** Requires setup, small learning curve

## Advanced Options

### ShipStation
**Best for:** High volume (50+ orders/month)
- **Cost:** Starts at $9.99/month
- **Features:** Advanced automation, inventory integration
- **Pros:** Powerful features, multi-carrier support
- **Cons:** Monthly cost, complex setup

### Shippo
**Best for:** Multi-carrier needs
- **Cost:** Pay-per-label or monthly plans
- **Features:** API access, multi-carrier comparison
- **Pros:** Compare rates across carriers
- **Cons:** Can be expensive for low volume

### EasyPost
**Best for:** Technical users, API integration
- **Cost:** Developer-focused pricing
- **Features:** API-first approach
- **Pros:** Highly customizable
- **Cons:** Requires technical knowledge

### Direct Carrier Accounts

#### UPS
- **Requirements:** Business account, daily volume
- **Benefits:** Better rates for high volume
- **Setup:** Call UPS business services

#### FedEx
- **Requirements:** Business account, regular shipping
- **Benefits:** Reliable service, good tracking
- **Setup:** Online application, approval process

## Step-by-Step Shipping Process

### Daily Shipping Workflow

#### Step 1: Review Orders
1. **Log into admin panel:** `superspec.studio/admin`
2. **Check new orders:** Look for "paid" status
3. **Print packing slips:** Click printer icon on each order
4. **Gather items:** Pull products from inventory

#### Step 2: Package Orders
1. **Choose appropriate packaging:**
   - **Small items:** Bubble mailers
   - **Fragile items:** Box with padding
   - **Clothing:** Poly mailers or boxes
2. **Package securely:**
   - Wrap items in tissue paper
   - Add protective padding
   - Seal package completely
3. **Weigh package:** Use scale for accurate weight

#### Step 3: Buy Shipping Label

#### Using Pirate Ship (Recommended)
1. **Go to pirateship.com**
2. **Click "Create a Shipment"**
3. **Enter customer address:**
   - Copy from admin panel
   - Double-check for accuracy
4. **Enter package details:**
   - Weight: From your scale
   - Dimensions: Measure package
   - Package type: Box, envelope, etc.
5. **Compare services:**
   - **Priority Mail:** 1-3 days, good value
   - **First-Class:** 2-5 days, cheapest for light items
   - **Ground:** 2-7 days, cheapest for heavy items
6. **Select service and pay**
7. **Print shipping label**

#### Using Post Office
1. **Take packaged order to post office**
2. **Tell clerk destination ZIP and weight**
3. **Select service (Priority, First-Class, etc.)**
4. **Pay for postage**
5. **Get receipt with tracking number**

#### Step 4: Ship Package
1. **Attach shipping label:**
   - Clear package surface
   - Place label on top
   - Cover with tape if needed
2. **Drop off package:**
   - **USPS:** Mailbox, post office, or schedule pickup
   - **UPS:** UPS Store or drop box
   - **FedEx:** FedEx Office or drop box
3. **Get tracking number** (if not already printed)

#### Step 5: Update Admin Panel
1. **Go to order in admin panel**
2. **Click "Mark Shipped"**
3. **Enter carrier:** USPS, UPS, FedEx
4. **Enter tracking number:** From shipping label
5. **Click "Confirm Shipment"**
6. **Customer automatically gets email** with tracking link

### Pro Tips for Efficiency

#### Batch Processing
1. **Process multiple orders at once**
2. **Print all labels together**
3. **Group by carrier for drop-off**
4. **Schedule pickups for larger batches**

#### Time Management
1. **Set daily shipping time:** Same time each day
2. **Prepare in advance:** Package ready orders
3. **Use templates:** Save common addresses
4. **Automate where possible:** Use saved package sizes

## International Shipping

### Current Setup
Your store is configured for **US-only shipping**. To add international:

#### Enable International Shipping
1. **Go to Admin → Settings → Shipping**
2. **Add international rates:**
   - **Canada:** $19.99 (Standard)
   - **UK/Europe:** $24.99 (Standard)
   - **Australia:** $29.99 (Standard)
3. **Update checkout:** International options appear

#### International Shipping Process
1. **Customs Forms:** Required for all international orders
2. **Customs Declarations:** Describe contents and value
3. **Duties/Taxes:** Customer pays these separately
4. **Tracking:** More limited for international

#### Recommended International Carriers
- **USPS International:** Most affordable, slower
- **UPS Worldwide:** Faster, more expensive
- **FedEx International:** Fastest, most expensive

## Shipping Best Practices

### Packaging Guidelines

#### Product-Specific Packaging
1. **Clothing Items:**
   - Fold neatly with tissue paper
   - Use poly mailers for flexibility
   - Include thank you card or branding

2. **Fragile Items:**
   - Double box if valuable
   - Use plenty of padding
   - Mark "Fragile" on outside

3. **Heavy Items:**
   - Use sturdy boxes
   - Reinforce bottom with extra tape
   - Consider double-walled boxes

#### Professional Presentation
1. **Branding elements:**
   - Custom packing tape with logo
   - Branded tissue paper
   - Thank you notes
   - Business cards

2. **Package organization:**
   - Place items neatly
   - Secure items to prevent shifting
   - Include packing slip

### Customer Experience

## Troubleshooting Shipping Issues

### Common Problems

#### Address Issues
1. **Incorrect address:**
   - Contact customer for correct address
   - Update order in admin panel
   - May need to purchase new shipping label

2. **Undeliverable address:**
   - Check with carrier for specific issue
   - Contact customer to resolve
   - May require address correction fee

#### Shipping Delays
1. **Carrier delays:**
   - Check carrier service alerts
   - Proactively notify customers
   - Consider shipping upgrades

2. **Weather delays:**
   - Monitor weather conditions
   - Communicate with customers
   - Document for insurance claims

#### Lost Packages
1. **File claim:**
   - Wait 7-14 days (carrier dependent)
   - File claim with carrier
   - Provide proof of value and shipping

2. **Customer resolution:**
   - Refund or reship order
   - Document for insurance
   - Review packaging process

#### Damaged Packages
1. **File damage claim:**
   - Take photos of damage
   - Keep packaging and contents
   - File promptly with carrier

2. **Prevent future damage:**
   - Review packaging methods
   - Add more protection
   - Consider insurance for valuable items

### Customer Service

#### Handling Shipping Inquiries
1. **Quick response:** Acknowledge within 24 hours
2. **Provide tracking:** Help customers track packages
3. **Explain delays:** Be honest about timeframes
4. **Offer solutions:** Reship or refund when appropriate

#### Returns and Exchanges
1. **Clear policy:** Display return policy prominently
2. **Easy process:** Provide return labels when possible
3. **Quick processing:** Handle returns promptly
4. **Good communication:** Keep customers informed

## Scaling Your Shipping

### When to Upgrade Services

#### Volume Indicators
- **25+ orders/month:** Consider shipping software
- **50+ orders/month:** Negotiated carrier rates
- **100+ orders/month:** Dedicated shipping staff
- **500+ orders/month:** Fulfillment service

#### Service Upgrades
1. **Shipping Software:** ShipStation, Shippo
2. **Fulfillment Services:** ShipBob, ShipMonk
3. **Carrier Accounts:** Direct UPS/FedEx accounts
4. **Warehouse Space:** Dedicated shipping area

### Automation Opportunities

#### Label Printing
- **Thermal printer:** Faster, no ink
- **Label integrations:** Print directly from admin
- **Batch printing:** Multiple labels at once

#### Order Processing
- **Barcode scanning:** Reduce manual entry
- **Order management:** Automated order routing
- **Inventory integration:** Automatic stock updates
